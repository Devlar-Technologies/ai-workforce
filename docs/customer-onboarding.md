- 3-5 goals across different pods to test breadth
- Focus on immediate business value opportunities
- Gradual increase in goal complexity

**Week 2 Goals:**
- 5-10 goals as team gains confidence
- Begin integrating with existing business workflows
- Start measuring ROI vs manual processes
